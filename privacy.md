Privacy Policy — My Gmail Grabber

Last updated: June 2026

My Gmail Grabber is a browser extension that downloads attachments from the Gmail email you currently have open, into a subfolder you choose.

What data the extension accesses
When you click "Grab All Attachments", the extension reads the attachment download links and file names from the email page that is open in your browser tab. This is done only at the moment you click the button, and only on mail.google.com.

What the extension does with that data
The attachment files are downloaded directly to your own computer, into your Downloads folder, using your browser's standard download mechanism.

What the extension does NOT do
- It does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to the developer or to any third party.
- It does not use any external server, analytics, or tracking.
- It does not read, store, or send the content of your emails.
- It does not access your Google account credentials.

All processing happens locally inside your browser. No information ever leaves your device except the attachment files, which are saved to your own Downloads folder.

Permissions used
- mail.google.com access — to read the attachment links from the open email.
- downloads — to save the attachment files to your Downloads folder.
- activeTab / scripting — to read the open email only when you click the button.
- storage — to briefly remember your chosen subfolder name during a download.

Contact
For questions about this extension, contact the developer at the email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing page.
